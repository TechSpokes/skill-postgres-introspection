# About

This page introduces the people and companies behind the `postgres-introspection` skill.

## TechSpokes

TechSpokes builds direct-booking technology for professional vacation-rental managers on TrackPMS, helping them grow direct revenue and reduce their dependence on online travel agencies. TechSpokes also authored the [Skill Base Template](https://github.com/TechSpokes/skill-base-template) used to generate this skill. Site: [www.techspokes.com](https://www.techspokes.com).

## LAWXER

LAWXER is an AI contract-analysis platform that reviews legal documents, scores their risk, flags critical clauses, and recommends edits, with AI assisting legal experts rather than replacing them. The database introspection method this skill teaches grew out of designing LAWXER's PostgreSQL-native data layer for legal-document analysis, where the real structure and security lived in the catalog and needed to be readable without the database. Site: [www.lawxer.ai](https://www.lawxer.ai).
